A workshop for the private sector reviews the most prominent features
of the Competition and procurement system

The Ministry of Finance and the Spending Efficiency Achievement Center, in
cooperation with the Local Content and Government Procurement Authority,
recently organized at the Chamber of Commerce in Riyadh, a workshop to
introduce the new government competition and procurement system, in the
presence of representatives from the private sector. The workshop touched
on the most prominent features of the new system, its objectives and scope
of application, in addition to the most important changes in it compared to
the previous system. The workshop also addressed the definition of the
provisions of the local content preference regulation, small and medium
enterprises, and companies listed in the financial market, and their role in the
development of the local economy. government competition and procurement
system, issued by royal decree, aims to enhance the effectiveness of financial
planning, raise the efficiency of spending on development projects, achieve
financial balance, prevent the influence of personal interests, enhance integrity
and competition, achieve the principle of equal opportunities, organize
procedures, and promote economic development. Achieving the best value and
protecting public money, and providing a fair treatment for competitors.
